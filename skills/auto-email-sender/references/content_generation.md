# Email Content Generation Guide

## Core Principle: Zero AI Tell-Tale Signs

Generated emails must read as human-written.

## What to Avoid

### 1. Bullet Points & Lists
BAD: "- research on VLA models\n- deployed gesture recognition"
GOOD: "I have been working on VLA models and tactile sensing. Before that I deployed gesture recognition systems."

### 2. Skill Listing
BAD: "Skills include: Python, PyTorch, ONNX..."
GOOD: "I use Python and PyTorch daily, and I have done ONNX exports and TensorRT optimization."

### 3. Perfect Grammar
BAD: Too mechanical
GOOD: Natural flow with occasional contractions

### 4. Generic Enthusiasm
BAD: "I would be thrilled to join your team..."
GOOD: "I have been following your work on autonomous driving in Singapore."

### 5. Formulaic Openings
BAD: "I am writing to express my strong interest in..."
GOOD: "I am reaching out about the Perception Engineer role."

## Chinese Template

Dear [Company] team,

Hello, I am Tian Lin. I am a MSc student at NTU Singapore graduating December 2026. I saw your [Position] opening and wanted to apply.

My research focuses on [topic]. Previously at [Company] I worked on [project].

I have been following [Company] work on [area] and my experience aligns well.

Resume attached.

Best,
Tian Lin
